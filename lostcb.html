Finding a lost Chromebook:

Once lost, Chromebooks can eb hard to find...

There are several places to look if you've lost you chromebook. There is no "Find my Chromebook" feature that can shortcut an old-fashioned search. Here is a list of places we think you should check, in order:

  1. Under your bed/ couch/ desk.
  2. In your old backpack (if you've just switched to a new one).
  3. In your classroom(s). Most of the time, if a student leaves their chromebook in a classroom, the teacher holds onto it for them.
  4. If a chromebook is lost in a common area of the High School, it may end up in the Tech Squad room.
