Try resetting your Infinite Campus password or the IChelp email.

The student login page for Infinite Campus will recover a username or let you reset a password.

Go to the student login page and click on the appropriate blue text under the login form.

This will allow you to work through a process to recover or reset what you've lost.

The IChelp email account is monitored by our Mayfield IT staff.

If the unsername and password processes didn't work, send them an email.

They can also help with other things, like correcting the spelling of a name.

The student-run Tech Squad is your first line of defense.

They are in the ChromeStore outside the HS cafeteria, nearly all periods.

If you learn anywhere else, your Building Point Person can work directly with you.

This website can also help you self-diagnose any problems, and work toward possible solutions.
