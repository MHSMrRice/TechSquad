Try resetting your Chrome Operating System on your own.

This process will return your chomebook to factory defaults, but your background and bookmarks will re-import from your online Google account.

Hit "ESC + Refresh + Power" to start the process.

Turn the Chromebook back on and then press "CTRL + D" next.

Then press "space" and "enter" as prompted.

When you see the welcome screen, follow the instructions to set-up your chromebook like it was brand new.

*Type the '@mayfieldstudents.org' end to your Google username, if the Chromebook tries '@mayfieldschools.org'*

The student-run Tech Squad is your first line of defense.

They are in the ChromeStore outside the HS cafeteria, nearly all periods.

If you learn anywhere else, your Building Point Person can work directly with you.

This website can also help you self-diagnose any problems, and work toward possible solutions.
